Our Story

About Us

Bearing in mind the constantly evolving and emerging tastes from around the world, Royal China Kolkata endeavours to reinvent Chinese cuisine in its entirety. We bring together the traditional and contemporary elements of Oriental culinary experience to reflect the gastronomic enrichments acquired over years of assimilation — using freshly sourced and genuine ingredients that culminate into a one-of-a-kind dining experience.

Over the years, the brand has broadened its horizons all across India and has received an overwhelming response from its patrons.

"The art of Chinese cooking is to make the meat taste like vegetables and the vegetables taste like meat, without either losing their original texture."

Head Chef, Royal China Kolkata
