Our Story

A New Vision of
Chinese Cuisine

Bearing in mind the constantly evolving and emerging tastes from around the world, Royal China Kolkata endeavours to reinvent Chinese cuisine in its entirety. We bring together the traditional and contemporary elements of Oriental culinary experience — reflecting gastronomic enrichments acquired over years of assimilation.


From freshly sourced and genuine ingredients to master chefs who craft delectable cuisines from the far East — it is where culinary skills meet the wizardry of modern-day gastronomic techniques.

"The art of Chinese cooking is to make the meat taste like vegetables and the vegetables taste like meat, without either losing their original texture."

— Head Chef, Royal China Kolkata
